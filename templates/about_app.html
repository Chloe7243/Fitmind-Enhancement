About FitMind

A smart wellness app for students to track their stress, exercises, and stay mindful.

What is FitMind?

FitMind is a digital companion built to help students stay consistent, focused, and energized throughout their academic journey. It offers tools for tracking stress levels, logging physical activities, practicing mindful breathing, and setting smart reminders — all in one seamless and motivating experience.
